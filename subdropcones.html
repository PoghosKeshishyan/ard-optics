Wedges

ARD-Optics manufactures wedges from sapphire, quartz glass and other optical materials. In addition to usual standard wedges we produce wedges of complicated configuration, for example segments, trapezoidal and other types at the request of the customer. The sizes of these elements range from several millimeters and end up to 80mm.
