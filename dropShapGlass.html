Sapphire and glass lightguides

Optical light guides are commonly used in medical applications for processing tissues, aiming to achieve therapeutic effects such as epilation, tattoo removal, and more.
ARD-Optics specializes in the manufacture of rectangular sapphire and optical glass light guides, all of which are meticulously polished on all surfaces, including the facets. To date, we have produced over a thousand light guides in various sizes, including 48x40x13mm, 20x20x50mm, 10x20x50mm, and 10x20x25mm.

In addition to sapphire parallelepiped light guides, we also produce optical light guides in custom shapes such as cylindrical, conical, pyramidal, and combined designs, upon customer request. Furthermore, we manufacture optical light guides made from quartz glass as well.
