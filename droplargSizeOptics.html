Large size optics

ARD-Optics manufactures big size sapphire and glass windows. The windows are rectangular (polygonal forms are available at the customer’s request) and round windows. All these windows can have wide facets of complicated configuration: stepped, and bilateral with different angles.

Materials: sapphire, fused silica (quartz) KU-1 (UV), KI (IR), glass BK-7
