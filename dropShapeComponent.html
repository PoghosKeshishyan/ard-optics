Sapphire components of irregular shape

Sapphire and glass products of irregular shape (cones, stepped windows and rods)

ARD-Optics manufactures optical polished items of complicated configuration from sapphire, quartz glass and other optical materials, in case of requirement with a high degree of flatness.

Optical products of irregular shapes can be divided into the following types:

  • Cones & truncated cones
  • Pyramids & truncated pyramids
  • Stepped windows
  • Prisms
  • Wedges
  • Sapphire Tubes
